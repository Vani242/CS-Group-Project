# 📊 COVID-19 Case and Death Rates by Vaccination Status

> A statistical analysis exploring the relationship between COVID-19 vaccination status and infection and mortality rates using hypothesis testing and descriptive statistics.

---

## 🌟 Highlights

* 📈 Analyzed COVID-19 case and death rates among vaccinated and unvaccinated populations
* 🧪 Applied independent two-sample t-tests to evaluate statistical significance
* 📊 Examined measures of central tendency and variability
* 🎲 Investigated sampling distributions and data trends
* 🧹 Performed data cleaning and validation
* 🩺 Evaluated the effectiveness of vaccination in reducing COVID-19 risks

---

## ℹ️ Overview

This project investigates the relationship between COVID-19 vaccination status and public health outcomes using the **Rates of COVID-19 Cases and Deaths by Vaccination Status** dataset.

The dataset contains **987 observations and 8 variables**, providing information on COVID-19 case and death rates among vaccinated and unvaccinated populations. Through statistical analysis, we explored whether vaccination status significantly influences infection and mortality rates.

To prepare the dataset for analysis, we checked for missing values and verified the accuracy of key variables such as the **Crude Vaccinated Incidence Rate (Crude vax IR)** and **Crude Unvaccinated Incidence Rate (Crude unvax IR)**.

---

## 🎯 Research Questions

1. Is there a significant difference in COVID-19 case rates between vaccinated and unvaccinated populations?

2. Is vaccination effective in reducing COVID-19 death rates?

---

## 🧪 Methodology

After cleaning and validating the dataset, we used descriptive statistics (central tendency and variability measures), sampling distributions, and independent two-sample t-tests to analyze differences in COVID-19 case and death rates between vaccinated and unvaccinated populations.

### Hypotheses

**Null Hypothesis (H₀):**
There is no significant difference between COVID-19 case and death rates among vaccinated and unvaccinated populations.

**Alternative Hypothesis (H₁):**
There is a significant difference between COVID-19 case and death rates among vaccinated and unvaccinated populations.
